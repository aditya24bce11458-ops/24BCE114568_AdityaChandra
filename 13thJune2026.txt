# Project Title

Campus Connect Portal

## Introduction

Campus Connect Portal is a console-based application developed using Spring Boot and MongoDB. It helps students create profiles, find mentors, and connect with seniors based on their academic year and role.

## Objectives

* To develop a console-based Campus Connect Portal.
* To allow students to create and manage their profiles.
* To provide a mentor-mentee interaction system.
* To perform CRUD operations using MongoDB.
* To demonstrate database relationships using MongoDB.

## Technologies Used

* Java (Spring Boot)
* MongoDB
* Swagger / Postman

## Main Modules

* Student Management
* Profile Management
* Role Management
* Mentor Request Management

## Database Relationships

* One-to-One: Student ↔ Profile
* One-to-Many: Role → Students
* Many-to-One: Students → Role
* Many-to-Many: Mentor ↔ Mentee

## Expected Outcome

The system will help students connect with mentors and seniors while demonstrating MongoDB CRUD operations and relationship mapping.
